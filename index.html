About FLaWeD

Initially, the idea for FLaWeD was to be a one-stop-shop for someone that doesn't have time to visit all their favorite websites in the day. I wanted to make it such that, upon logging in, the user would see an (at least semi-) live representation of what was on the websites they would look at every day all in one place. They would even be able to schedule a refresh time for the site so they would know if something had changed since the last time they checked it. Thus comes the name ... FLaWeD stands for "Favorite Live Web Database". Then there is the social media aspect: "Share your FlAWs!" Using Facebook and Twitter, the user would be able to show other people what they were up to.

As you can see, it's nowhere close to that level, but I hope that you can see what I was shooting for.
